# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code and ideas.
- Assume good intent and collaborate in good faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, hostile language, or intimidation
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participation for behavior that violates this code.
